About GGSIPU

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) is a public, professional university located in Delhi, India. Established in 1998, it is renowned for its focus on professional and technology-oriented education. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programmes, and is committed to developing skilled professionals ready for the industry.
